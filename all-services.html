Ride Sharing

Ride Sharing is perfect for individuals and small groups who want to save money while reducing their carbon footprint. By sharing rides with others heading in the same direction, you not only split the cost but also help decrease traffic congestion and emissions. Our drivers are thoroughly vetted, and our vehicles are regularly inspected to guarantee your safety and comfort throughout the journey.

Future Ride

Future Ride allows you to plan your transportation needs ahead of time. Whether you have an important meeting, a flight to catch, or a special event to attend, you can book your ride up to 7 days in advance. Simply select your desired date and time, choose from a variety of vehicle options, and let us take care of the rest. Our reliable service ensures that your car will arrive promptly at your specified location, providing you with peace of mind and a stress-free experience.

Intercity Service

Intercity Service is designed for long-distance travel between cities. Whether you're planning a business trip, a family visit, or a weekend getaway, our intercity service offers a comfortable and convenient way to reach your destination. With a range of vehicle options to choose from, you can select the perfect ride for your journey. Our professional drivers are experienced in intercity travel and will ensure a smooth and enjoyable ride.

Group Rides

Group Rides are ideal for friends, family, or colleagues traveling together. Whether you're heading to an event, going on an outing, or simply commuting together, our group ride service provides a spacious and comfortable vehicle to accommodate multiple passengers. Enjoy the convenience of traveling together while sharing the cost of the ride. Our drivers are skilled in managing group dynamics and ensuring a pleasant experience for all passengers.

Package Delivery

Package Delivery is a reliable and efficient service for transporting packages and documents. Whether you need to send a gift, important paperwork, or any other item, our package delivery service ensures that your items are handled with care and delivered on time. We offer various delivery options to suit your needs, including same-day and next-day delivery. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and keeping you informed throughout the delivery process.

Family Ride

Family Rides offer a safe and comfortable transportation option for families. Our family-friendly vehicles are equipped with child safety seats and ample space for luggage and strollers. Whether you're going on a family outing, visiting relatives, or heading to the airport, our experienced drivers will ensure a smooth and enjoyable ride for you and your loved ones. We prioritize safety and comfort to make your family travel experience stress-free.
